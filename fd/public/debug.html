Troubleshooting Steps:

Step 1: Click "Get from localStorage" to check your current stored token
Step 2: If role shows "user" instead of "admin", you need to logout and login again
Step 3: Click "Clear localStorage" to remove old tokens
Step 4: Go back to the app and login again with your admin credentials
